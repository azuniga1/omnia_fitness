About OMNIA Fitness


OMNIA fitness is a unique personal training studio, where we pride ourselves in helping you seek out and unleash your greatest potential.

Currently located in the beautiful beach town of Nha Trang OMNIA Fitness represents the premier brand of personal and group training. We are neither a corporate mega gym, nor a small neighborhood gym. we are a class and style of our own, with 20+ years of experience an knowledge we set the international standard of quality personal training and group class programs in Nha Trang, Vietnam.

With experience and dedication to the fitness industry, our team of fitness experts are excited and eager to help aid your healthy journey.




Our Philosophy


MISSION: You may feel nervous to step out of your comfort zone, but that is the beauty of a successful fitness program here at OMNIA Fitness; you are never alone in the journey.


It may not be an easy road ahead but will certainly be one worth taking. We love to inspiring people to reach their full potential, inside and outside of the gym. Through individual program design, we will ensure your specific needs are effectively and professionally catered to. OMNIA fitness is honored to aid you in your personal journey towards overall health and happiness.
